About BDO SmartBank

BDO SmartBank is a fresh and user-friendly redesign of Banco de Oro, one of the Philippines’ most trusted financial institutions. We’re combining over two decades of solid banking experience with the power of today’s technology.

Founded in 1998, BDO has consistently been a leader in customer trust, financial growth, and innovation. Today, SmartBank continues that legacy — now focused on giving every Filipino fast, convenient, and secure digital banking solutions.

What makes us different?

  • ✔ We put technology and security at the heart of your banking experience.
  • ✔ Our services are designed to be accessible to all, regardless of background or location.
  • ✔ We believe in real human support — chat, call, or visit us anytime you need help.
